Rules of Engagement

This game combines the classic "Rock, Paper, Scissors" with additional elements "Lizard" and "Spock," and adds a twist by involving celebrities from various professions. The player selects a profession and a celebrity from that profession, then plays multiple rounds of "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock" against the computer, which also selects a random celebrity.


First to 10 rounds (including tie rounds), will win the game!
